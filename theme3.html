Maximize Returns, with Prudence


Established in 2011, Symphony Ventures Pte Ltd (“Symphony”) is a private equity firm whose primary strength and focus lie in Shipping, Renewables and Oil and Gas services. Helmed by a professional management with a proven track record in these specialized industries, Symphony identifies, invests in and enhances the value of businesses with demonstrated potential.

This is evidenced by our successful running of the well-established Barracuda Ventures Pte Ltd (“Barracuda”) since 2001, which held several subsidiaries serving the oil, gas and marine industries, before it was acquired by a Fortune 500 conglomerate in 2011. Our vision is to create a sustainable cash flow with integrity and transparency. Above all, we believe in having fun while creating a business success.

Business

Symphony Ventures embarked on a fleet building programme consisting of modern, futuristic vessels with unique features in a German shipyard. The new building programme consisted of Eight Vessels.

The Technical Management of the vessels is being taken care by our Subsidiary Symphony Shipping B.V. in Holland.
