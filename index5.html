Maximize Returns, with Prudence

Established in 2011, Symphony Ventures Pte Ltd (“Symphony”) is a private equity firm whose primary strength and focus lie in Shipping, Renewables and Oil and Gas services. Helmed by a professional management with a proven track record in these specialized industries, Symphony identifies, invests in and enhances the value of businesses with demonstrated potential. This is evidenced by our successful running of the well-established Barracuda Ventures Pte Ltd (“Barracuda”) since 2001, which held several subsidiaries serving the oil, gas and marine industries, before it was acquired by a Fortune 500 conglomerate in 2011. Our vision is to create a sustainable cash flow with integrity and transparency. Above all, we believe in having fun while creating a business success.

  • Mantis Vision Ltd

    In June 2012, Symphony Ventures acquired a significant stake in Mantis Vision Ltd, Israel. Mantis Vision using its proprietary core technology actualized the handheld 3D camera, which can be used to acquire accurate 3D modeling of highly cluttered or dynamic scenes

  • Economos Singapore Pte Ltd

    A joint venture with SKF Economos Austria Gmbh, Economos manufactures a wide range of high quality seals and related products which have been field proven in numerous industries, including steel, food processing and automotive.

  • Blue Star Coating Pte Ltd

    Barracuda ventured into sand blasting and surface treatment services with the incorporation of Blue Star in 2004. With one of the largest and the most technologically advanced coating, blasting and spraying facilities in Southeast Asia, Blue Star is an industry leader in metal treatment services.

  • Euro Offshore Pte Ltd

    Barracuda founded Euro Offshore in 2002 with a majority stake to trade in oilfield equipment. Euro Offshore specialised in the purchasing of used BOPs, refurbishing them to API quality and marketing them to drilling contractors. It also stocks replacement parts for Cameron, Hydril and Shaffer.

  • STSA Pte Ltd

    Acquired by Barracuda in 2001, STSA became a respected leader in the oil and gas industry focusing on the refurbishment of Blowout Preventers. The company’s sterling reputation as the Specialist in Manufacturing, Re-manufacturing and Re-conditioning of high pressure drill floor equipment earned the respect and loyalty of customers at home and abroad, on land and offshore.

  • STSA India Pvt Ltd

    Incorporated in 2009, STSA India is a sister company of STSA Singapore providing services in manufacturing,remanufacturing and reconditioning Blowout Preventers for oil exploration & production and drilling companies operating in India and the Middle-East.

  • QBOP Pte Ltd

    Founded by Barracuda in 2007,QBOP specialized in the design and marketing of new BOPs for surface stacks, and has successfully marketed to world renowned drilling companies.
     

  • Symphony Marine Equity II Pte Ltd

    In Feb 2012, Symphony Ventures floated this SPC and has contracted to acquire a second 10800 bhp AHTS, currently under a long term bareboat charter to a leading group in Australia. Symphony Marine Equity II (SME II) was incorporated as joint ventures with RS Platou Finans, part of the leading international ship and offshore brokers RS Platou. In the beginning of 2013, Symphony Ventures has acquired the stake of RS Platou Finans and thus the above company is fully owned by Symphony Ventures. In Dec 2013, Symphony Ventures exited by selling the entire stake held in SME II.
